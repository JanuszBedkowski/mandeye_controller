# Wiring 

## Wiring connection to Livox MID360
### Pinout of M12A12FL12AFLSD001
Pins numbers, in plug, from documentation : [link](https://eu.mouser.com/datasheet/2/18/Amphenol_02112019_M12AXXFL-12AFL-SXXXX(A)-1531779.pdf)

| Pin | Color  | Function |
|-----|--------|----------|
|  1  | White  | Power +  |
|  9  | Black  | Power +  |
|  2  | Brown  | Ground   |
|  3  | Green  | Ground   |
|  4  | Yellow | ETH-TX+  |
|  5  | Gray   | ETH-TX-  |
|  6  | Pink   | ETH-RX+  |
|  7  | Blue   | ETH-RX-  |
|  8  | Red    | PPS      |
| 10  | Violet | NMEA     |
| 11  | Gray/Pink | N/A   |
| 12  | Gray/Pink | N/A   |
